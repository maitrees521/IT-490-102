About Covidology

Purpose:

Purpose of the project is to educate the general public about coronavirus and how to not catch it by creating
a game where the character is placed in a sensitive environment considering coronavirus. The player will have
various options to choose from and their decision reflects if they have won or lost the game. Another purpose of
the project is also to educate the next generation on how to prevent another pandemic from happening.

Game Play:

Players navigate through the map. The player has an exposure meter. When navigating through the map the meter will go up. If the meter is full they have been fully exposed to Covid-19. Being fully exposed means the player loses the game. NPCs around the map will give players tasks, when completed they gain experience points. With enough experience points the players can level up. Leveling up gives players perks. Perks will slow down the exposure meter.

Level 1: Mask
Level 2: Vaccine 1
Level 3: Vaccine 2
Level 4: Booster
